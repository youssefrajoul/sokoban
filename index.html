How to Play?

  • You are in control of the Player. The Player can move up, down, left and right:
  • Arrow keys for movement.
  • Space for next Level.
  • Restart for reset.

Rules:

Only one box can be pushed at a time.

A box cannot be pulled X.

The player cannot walk through boxes or walls.

The puzzle is solved when all boxes are on their corresponding goals.
